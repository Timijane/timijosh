FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Triangletech's services and training programs.

What technology training programs does Triangletech offer?+
Triangletech offers comprehensive training programs in Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Next.js), Mobile App Development (Flutter, React Native), Cybersecurity (Ethical Hacking, Network Security, Penetration Testing), Data Analysis (Excel, SQL, Power BI), AI & Automation (ChatGPT, Prompt Engineering, AI Tools), UI/UX Design (Figma, Wireframing, Prototyping), and Digital Marketing.
Does Triangletech provide technology solutions for businesses?+
Yes, Triangletech provides professional technology solutions including website development, mobile app development, custom software development, cybersecurity services, SEO optimization, cloud solutions, digital marketing, and technology consulting for businesses of all sizes.
Are the training programs suitable for beginners?+
Absolutely! Our training programs are designed for all levels — beginner, intermediate, and advanced. Each course clearly indicates its level, and our instructors provide personalized mentorship to ensure every student succeeds regardless of their starting point.
How long do the training programs take?+
Training program durations vary: Web Development (12 weeks), Mobile App Development (14 weeks), Cybersecurity (16 weeks), Data Analysis (10 weeks), AI & Automation (8 weeks), and UI/UX Design (10 weeks). All programs include flexible learning options.
Do I receive a certificate after completing a training program?+
Yes, all Triangletech training programs include a certificate of completion. Our certificates are recognized and demonstrate your practical skills and knowledge to potential employers.
What is the cost of training programs?+
Our training programs are competitively priced to ensure accessibility. We offer flexible payment plans, early-bird discounts, and scholarship opportunities. Contact us directly for specific program pricing and current promotions.
Do you offer remote or online training options?+
Yes! All our training programs are available in both in-person and fully remote formats. Our online training includes live interactive sessions, recorded lectures, hands-on labs, and one-on-one mentorship — giving you the flexibility to learn from anywhere.
How can I contact Triangletech for business solutions?+
You can reach us through WhatsApp, email, LinkedIn, or by scheduling a consultation directly on our website. Visit our contact section for all available channels. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.
