Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the next batch start? +
Batch enrollment opens periodically throughout the year. Check the announcements section above or join our waitlist to be notified when the next batch opens.
Can I join a batch after it has started? +
No. Batches are cohort-based for the best learning experience. Once enrollment closes, you'll need to wait for the next batch.
How do referrals work? +
Share your unique referral link with friends. When someone enrolls through your link, you earn 20% commission on their payment. Commission is withdrawable to your bank account.
Do I get a certificate? +
Yes! You earn a certificate after completing each stage (Elementary, Advanced, Professional). If you complete all three stages, you earn a final professional certificate. All certificates are shareable on social media.
What if I miss a lesson? +
All lessons are pre-recorded and available 24/7 within your batch period. You can learn at your own pace, but we encourage keeping up with the cohort for the best experience.
