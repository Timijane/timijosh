Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the next batch start? +
Batch enrollment opens periodically. Check the announcements section or join our community for updates.
Can I join a batch after it has started? +
No. Batches are cohort-based. Once enrollment closes, you'll need to wait for the next batch.
How do referrals work? +
Share your unique link. When someone enrolls, you earn 20% commission. Withdrawable to your bank account.
Do I get a certificate? +
Yes! Earn a certificate after each stage. All certificates are shareable on social media.
What if I miss a lesson? +
All lessons are pre-recorded and available 24/7 within your batch period. Learn at your pace.
